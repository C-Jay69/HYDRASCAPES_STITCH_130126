Maldives Luxury Escape

location_on North Malé Atoll, Maldives

Experience Paradise

Escape to the breathtaking islands of the Maldives. Our luxury package offers an all-inclusive stay at a 5-star resort, complete with private villa access, world-class dining, and exclusive underwater excursions. From the moment you land, you'll be greeted with unmatched hospitality and the soothing sounds of the Indian Ocean.

Your Journey

7 Days / 6 Nights
1

Arrival & Sunset Dinner

Meet our representative at Velana International Airport followed by a scenic speed-boat transfer to your resort. Enjoy a welcome drink and a 4-course sunset dinner by the beach.

2

Snorkeling Safari

Discover the vibrant coral reefs and swim alongside sea turtles in a guided snorkeling tour. Afternoon free for leisure at the infinity pool.

3

Spa & Wellness Day

Indulge in a signature 90-minute Balinese massage at the overwater spa. Spend your evening watching a traditional Bodu Beru performance.
